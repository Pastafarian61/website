Welcome to The Baked Bean


Welcome to The Baked Bean coffee house where we strive to bring you the best tasting beverages at competative prices.
Our business is a local, family friendly store run by the same family for nearly 4 generations now.
Now we may not be a major conglomerate like Starbecks or Cafe Pero, but with our patented house blend will leave you calm, collected, & keen to start your day right.
Hot beverages not your thing? Well chill your beans with a selection of iced teas & soft drinks for the kids.
Still not satisfied? Feeling peckish? Well don't worry, we've got you covered as we also offer a selection of tasty treats to fill the stomachs of even the most picky of eaters.
